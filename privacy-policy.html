Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 7, 2025

At Edwin's Picks, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services, including our integration with Pinterest's API.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you interact with our site or connect via Pinterest, we may collect your name, email address, or Pinterest profile information (e.g., username, profile ID) with your explicit consent through Pinterest's OAuth authentication.
  • Non-Personal Information: We collect usage data such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and website interactions (e.g., pages visited, links clicked) via cookies or analytics tools.
  • Affiliate Data: When you click affiliate links (e.g., Amazon Affiliate Program), we may collect anonymized data about your clicks or purchases to track commissions, as permitted by our affiliate partners.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Provide personalized product recommendations via our website and Pinterest integration.
  • Facilitate affiliate marketing programs, such as tracking clicks or purchases through Amazon Affiliate Program links.
  • Improve our website's functionality and user experience through analytics.
  • Communicate with you, if you opt in, about updates or promotions.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service Providers: Third-party services like Pinterest (for API integration) or analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics) that help us operate our site.
  • Affiliate Partners: Anonymized data may be shared with affiliate programs like Amazon to track referrals.
  • Legal Compliance: If required by law, we may disclose information to comply with legal obligations.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience and track affiliate link activity. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.

5. Pinterest API Usage

Our app integrates with Pinterest's API to allow you to save or share our product recommendations. When you authenticate via Pinterest, we access only the data you explicitly allow (e.g., profile information or Pins). We store this data securely and use it solely to enhance your experience on our platform.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of data collection (e.g., by disabling cookies or revoking Pinterest app permissions).
  • Contact us at privacy@edwinslifestylepicks.com for data-related requests.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your data, but no system is completely secure. We encourage you to use strong passwords and secure connections.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

9. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at privacy@edwinslifestylepicks.com.
